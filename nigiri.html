Nigiri

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Sushi rice or Japanese short grain rice
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 Â½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • Vegetable oil or canola oil
  • Sashimi grade fish/seafood of your choice
  1. Make your sushi rice. Take your sushi rice and rinse it well until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook your rice according to the package instructions over the stovetop, or in a rice cooker.
  3. In a bowl, make your vinegar mixture by whisking salt, sugar and rice wine vinegar. Pour over your cooked rice and fluff, mix until fully combined.
  4. Assemble your nigiri using sashmi of your choice

    Tips

  • From my experience, make sure to shape the sushi rice with wet hands so that the rice doesn't stick to your hands as much
  • I like to chil the rice in the fridge for a while to make sure that the rice is easily molded.
