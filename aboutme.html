About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Dylan Hugo Sajino and welcome to my food blog! I am an avid eater and thus what better way to eat good food than cooking your own! My love for cooking started when I wanted to lose more weight. I realised that a lot of so called "diet food" is very bland and I thought that it would never be sustainable for me to keep eating "diet food" to lose weight and achieve my fitness goals. That is when I thought of cooking my own food so that I can control I can track my calories and macronutrients intake through my own cooked food.
Makan bareng means to eat together in Indonesian. In this blog, I will be uploading my own simple recipes that have saved me when I dont have much time to cook and even more complicated recipes that require more time to show off your friends/family that you can cook. If you are a college student who wants simple recipes to cook with your limited time or someone who is looking for recipes to spice up their cooking, you have come to the right place.

Have fun and "mari kita makan bareng".
