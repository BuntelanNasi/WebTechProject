Indomie

Ingredients:

  • 2 Indomie Packets (I feel one isnt enough)
  • 1 egg
  • Kecap manis
  • Sambal ABC
  • Suseno
  • Fried shallots
  • Gochujang (preference)
  1. Cut packet and pour sauces into bowl. I would put extra kecap manis and sambal abc for taste. When Im feeling extra brave, I like to add gochujang to my indomie as well.
  2. Cook the indomie noodles per packet instructions. However, I like my noodles firm so I usually taste them before taking them out.
  3. After noodles are cooked, dry and transfer into the bowl to mix it with the sauce.
  4. Fry an egg with oil on high temp to make it crispy but the yolk runny at the same time.
  5. Serve with fried egg and addtional toppings of fried shallots and suseno on top.

    Tips

  • You can add additional protein if you feel that the dish is lacking in macronutrients. This is more of a midnight snack than a meal. I recommend fried chicken or spam as good protein choices.
  • If you want the noodles firm and don't want to try it before taking it out, cook the noodles 10-20 seconds earlier than packet instructions.
